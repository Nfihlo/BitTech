Option 2: Platform-backed

“Data from platforms like Coursera and edX shows that only about 5–10% of learners complete short online courses.”

Who is affected?

Millions of learners worldwide—especially young people and job seekers—who enroll in short online courses on platforms like Coursera and edX but never complete them.

What’s currently going wrong?

Most short courses are not engaging enough. Learners lose motivation, feel disconnected, and drop out. There is:

Little interaction or fun in the learning process

No strong incentives to keep going

Limited engagement, making learning feel like a chore

As a result, even though people start with good intentions, they lose interest and stop learning halfway.




Our Solution


We are building a gamified learning app that helps learners better understand difficult concepts while staying engaged.

How it works:

While using learning platforms like Coursera or edX, learners can highlight a concept they don’t understand.

Our app then automatically generates a tailored game based on that specific concept.

The learner plays the game, making learning interactive, fun, and easier to understand.

What makes it different:

Personalized learning: every game is custom-made for the learner’s problem

Learning through play: turns difficult topics into engaging challenges

Instant support: no need to search for explanations elsewhere

Why it works:
Instead of learners getting frustrated and dropping out, they stay engaged because:

They actively participate in learning

They enjoy the process

They understand concepts faster

In one sentence:
“We turn confusion into gameplay—helping learners understand concepts and stay committed to finishing their courses.”
